BORN V1.2
Born is a humanistic typeface that is based on traditional calligraphic forms, but with some new features in its endings, strokes and drops, that provide a more open, fresh and actual look. It also has a big X height, so it offers a high legibility even at small sizes.
http://www.carlosdetoro.com/born

AUTHOR
Carlos de Toro is a graphic designer from Logroño (Spain) now(2013) based in Barcelona, and a type lover, Born is his first typography.

BORN V1.2
Born es una tipografía humanista que sigue las formas caligráficas tradicionales en su base pero con algunas novedades en sus remates, trazos y gotas que aportan un aspecto más abierto, fresco y actual. Además posee una elevada altura de x, de modo que ofrece una alta legibilidad incluso a tamaños pequeños
http://www.carlosdetoro.com/born-en
